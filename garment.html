Garment Design

Fashion / Wearables / Apparel

salvi.world/garment
✕ − □

Apparel and wearable design spanning hoodies, outerwear, and footwear concepts. Each piece is built around the same visual language as the broader brand work — heavy graphics, strong silhouettes, and garments that feel like they belong to a world.

The Hurakan hoodie series and original boot sketches push the design into 3D object territory — where clothing becomes sculpture.
