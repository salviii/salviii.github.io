art i make for myself, started making these in 2020 mid pandemic and it continues to be my most personal medium of artistic expression. many stages of my life and development as a person are chronicled through these pieces.

everything is made with blender (3D modeling and sculpting program) which i use to make a sort of "digital still life" and scenes, then i composite different pieces of my 3D scenes in photoshop (image editing), and then i use procreate (digital drawing and painting) to paint and sketch over each image.

no artificial intelligence or image generation programs were used for any of these works, ai has its uses but definetly not when it comes to human expression.
