Syd

Album Artwork / Packaging / Visual Identity

salvi.world/syd
✕ − □

Album artwork, vinyl packaging, and tour visuals for Syd. The project covers the full visual identity around the release — from the primary album cover to physical packaging, sleeve design, and promotional materials.

The work needed to feel personal and intimate while also living at scale — on streaming platforms, on vinyl, and on stage.


project images:

The vinyl packaging brings the visual system into physical form — sleeve, inner sleeve, and disc label all working as one cohesive object. Designed to feel like something worth keeping.

The tour poster extends the visual identity into the live context — translating the album's mood into something that works in a venue, on a wall, in a hand.
