growing up bilingual (spanish/english) i always had a particular interest in language and how living things communicate. i mainly attribute this to my need to really study both instead of just being able to absorb them. i love etymology, and where linguistics intersects with neuroscience and anthropology.

about 2 years ago i started developing my own writing system, i had been obsessed with ancient writing and the history of written language and the purpose it served as the basis of government, religion, and the basis for extended human collaboration and coexistence (culture). if we are the hardware, language is the software that runs on our operating system (conscious/unconscious).

the writing system consists of a deconstructed latin alphabet. i wanted it to look like how whispers sound when you are just far away enough to hear it as just air and vibrations shaped by the speakers lips.
